How to Play?

The object of the game is to match the Parent Animal with the Baby Animal with the least amount of moves and as quick as possible.

The player needs to select two cards, first followed by a second. If the animals are part of the same family, they will remain turned.

When the player turns over two cards, the number of moves increases.

You will receive a score depending on the number of moves and time taken to complete.
